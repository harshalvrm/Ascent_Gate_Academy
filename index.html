Common Question

1. What is the validity of this course ?

Courses are provided in two variants for 1 year and 2 year validity.

2. What is medium of communication during lectures ?

Lectures are in easy Hinglish (Hindi and English mix) language.

3. What are the softwares required and from where I can get this ?

Software for Windows and app for Android mobile which is required will be provided along with this course.

4. Will I get Lifetime support with Lifetime validity product ?

Lifetime validity is not offered by us now. However previously those who have taken course with Lifetime validity means you can access the lectures Lifetime in single system. Support is not Lifetime. You will get support up to latest two GATE exam. (i.e. if you have subscribed in 2018, support will be up to GATE 2020 only). If you get any problem after that, we cannot help as our softwares are getting updated every year
